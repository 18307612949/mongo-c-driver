bson_init()¶

Synopsis¶

void
bson_init (bson_t *b);

Parameters¶

  • b: A bson_t.

Description¶

The bson_init() function shall initialize a bson_t that is placed on the stack. This is equivalent to initializing a bson_t to BSON_INITIALIZER.
